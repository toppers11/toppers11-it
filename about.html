About Toppers11

Built for Income Tax Department employees. Only.

Toppers11 is India's only mock test platform designed exclusively for the Income Tax Departmental Promotion Examinations — MS (UDC), ITI (Inspector) and ITO (Officer) — with paper-wise tests aligned to the official CBDT syllabus.

Our Mission

Why We Built Toppers11

Every year, thousands of dedicated Income Tax Department employees appear for their departmental promotion examinations — MS, ITI and ITO. Yet for years, there was no dedicated online platform that offered a realistic, paper-pattern mock test experience aligned to the CBDT-prescribed syllabus.

Toppers11 was created to fill that gap. We understand the unique challenges of departmental exams: the negative marking, the tight time limits, the specific chapter weightages, and the importance of previous-year question patterns. Our platform is built around these realities, not around generic exam prep.

100% Exam-Aligned

Every question bank and test series is built on the official CBDT Departmental Examination Rules 2009 syllabus — no generic content, no filler. What you practice is exactly what appears in the exam.

Real Exam Interface

Our test player replicates the Online Mode exam interface — section-wise navigation, colour-coded question palette, mark-for-review, negative marking (1/8th) — so exam day feels familiar.

AI-Powered Questions

Our 2,000+ question bank is curated from previous years' papers (2009–2024) and enhanced with AI-generated practice questions — covering every chapter, every weightage band.

For Department Insiders

Built by Income Tax professionals who have gone through the same examinations. We know the pain points — the service rule nuances, the procedural questions, the allied law traps — and our tests reflect that insider knowledge.
